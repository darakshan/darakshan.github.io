Day 80 - Sunday 3/06/2016 I was up at 5:30, determined this time to find the early morning chanting. It was dark, misty, and pleasantly warm as I left the guest house. I could hear gongs, voices, and the distinctive sound of deep throat chanting in the distance. I walked past the empty Drepung Loseling temple and though the courtyard where a few monks were walking and chanting, toward the chanting sounds, into the street.
